The Fat

We chose Wagyu Femur over generic beef. The high oleic acid content creates a naturally sweet, aromatic lipid layer that requires less chemical modification.

The Texture

We rejected the standard "boil knuckle" method in favor of the Tendon Blitz. Immersion blending 4lbs of cooked tendons creates a permanent "latte-like" viscosity that feels luxurious.

The Tare

We selected Shiro Shoyu (White) over the Beef Jus (Dark). Dark soy turns Paitan grey. White soy preserves the "Wagyu Gold" aesthetic.
